About Microdynamics

Established in 1988, Microdynamics specializes in developing products in vehicular surveying and measurement industries, including, but not limited to, department of transportation, forestry, construction, paving, and even hobbyists. We are constantly improving and developing new products to best utilize the latest technological developments. Our Distance Measuring Instruments are among the best performing, easiest to use, and versatile in the industry.
